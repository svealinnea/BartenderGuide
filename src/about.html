About

The Bartender's Guide has already supplanted the great Codex Bartactica as the standard repository of all bar/pub/saloon/watering-hole knowledge and wisdom, for though it has many omissions, it scores over the older, more pedestrian work in two important respects: First, it is cheaper; and secondly it has the words CALM DOWN inscribed in large friendly letters on its cover.

The Bartender's Guide to the Galaxy is an indispensable companion to all those who are keen to make sense of bar life in an infinitely complex and confusing multi-bar-verse. Where it is inaccurate it is at least definitely inaccurate. In cases of major discrepancy it's always reality that's got it wrong.

"The Guide is definitive. Reality is often inaccurate."
